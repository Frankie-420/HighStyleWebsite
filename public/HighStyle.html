AUSTRALIAN QUALITY KITCHENS

At High Style Furniture and Kitchens we provide a range of expert design consultancy services for residential and commercial clients across Inverell, Moree, Bingara, Glen Innes and the surrounding areas. We’ve also completed projects as far spread as Townsville, Adelaide, Coffs Harbour and Bourke. As a proudly owned and operated family business, we’ve breathed new life into homes and workplaces since 1992. We employ the latest in digital technology to give your residential or commercial design project the attention it deserves.
